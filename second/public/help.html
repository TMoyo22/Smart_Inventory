Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I add new products to the inventory?

To add new products, go to the 'Products In-Stock' page and click the 'Add Product' button. Fill in the necessary details and click 'Submit'.

How can I update product information?

You can update product details by navigating to the 'Products In-Stock' page, selecting a product, and editing its information.

What should I do if a product is expiring soon?

Expiring products will be listed under the 'Expiring Soon' section on the dashboard. You can choose to take action by clicking on the product for more details.
